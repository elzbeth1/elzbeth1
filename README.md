## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 🏥 Healthcare Insurance Premium Prediction
> *Python · XGBoost · Feature Engineering · Streamlit*

Built an end-to-end premium prediction system that goes beyond surface-level accuracy — diagnosing *why* a model fails on specific segments and fixing it.

| Metric | Result |
|---|---|
| Overall Model Accuracy | **R² = 0.98** |
| Young Patient Segment (before fix) | R² ≈ 0.60, 73% high-error rate |
| After Genetic Risk Feature Engineering | Error rate dropped to **2%** (97% reduction) |
| Deployment | Live Streamlit app with dynamic model selection |

🔑 *Key insight: Aggregate metrics hid a critical segment failure. Engineered a domain-specific feature to fix it — not just tune hyperparameters.*

---

### 🚗 Car Damage Classification (Computer Vision)
> *PyTorch · ResNet · EfficientNet · Transfer Learning · FastAPI · Optuna*

Multi-class car damage classifier using CNNs and transfer learning, with a full-stack deployment pipeline.

| Metric | Result |
|---|---|
| Validation Accuracy | **82%+** (outperformed from-scratch CNN baseline) |
| Architecture | ResNet + EfficientNet with Optuna hyperparameter search |
| Deployment | Streamlit frontend + FastAPI backend for real-time inference |

🔑 *Key insight: Systematic architecture comparison + automated HPO via Optuna beat manual tuning significantly.*

---

### 💳 Credit Risk Prediction — Loan Default Classification
> *Python · Logistic Regression · XGBoost · Scikit-learn · Streamlit*

Binary classification on a highly imbalanced financial dataset, optimised for real-world business risk tolerance.

| Metric | Result |
|---|---|
| Primary Metric (Recall) | **0.94** — minimises missed defaults |
| Secondary Metrics | ROC-AUC + KS Statistic monitored |
| Business Value | Deployed Streamlit app for non-technical credit risk scoring |

🔑 *Key insight: Chose Recall over Accuracy deliberately — a missed default costs far more than a false alarm in finance.*

---

### 📊 Credit Card Launch Strategy & A/B Testing Analysis
> *Python · Statistical Testing · Customer Segmentation · EDA*

Data-driven go-to-market strategy for a credit card launch, combining behavioural analytics with rigorous A/B testing.

| Step | Approach |
|---|---|
| Customer Segmentation | Behavioural analysis to identify high-potential segments |
| Product Validation | Hypothesis testing on A/B test groups |
| Business Outcome | Demonstrated **statistically significant increase** in customer spending |

🔑 *Key insight: Bridged the gap between data analysis and business strategy — segmentation informed targeting, A/B testing validated it.*
